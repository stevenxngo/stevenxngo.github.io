Hi there, I'm Steven, an aspiring software engineer.


I'm a student in Computer Science with a concentration in Software at Northeastern University, where I plan on graduating in 2024. I am also an incoming Software Engineer intern at Wise Systems, a startup located in Cambridge, MA, where I will assist in projects that enable new capabilities in delivery and logistical capacities. Some of my greatest technical skills are in Java, Python, HTML and JavaScript, all of which I will be using at Wise and hope to utilize in the future!

Whether it be something as minuscule as word searches and the fastest way to complete them or a technical problem given in my CS class in high school I have found joy in developing an efficient solution to solve said problem. Thus, computer science offers me a clear avenue to implement and expand my work to make a greater difference in the world, while also exposing me to new ideas and people, just like yourself!
